Lentil stew

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 garlic
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • salt, pepper
  • 1 cup dried lentils
  1. Stir onion on oil until yellow
  2. Add chopped carrots and a garlic
  3. Separately cook lentil for 20 minutes until cooked
  4. Put cooked lentils together with onion and carrots and mix well
  5. Season according to the flawor
