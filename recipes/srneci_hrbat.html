Roe deer back

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 0.15 kg wallnuts
  • 3 spoons bread crumbs
  • 0.1l rum
  • 0.15kg sugar
  • 0.1kg dark chocolate
  1. Mix egg yolks and sugar until smooth
  2. Beat egg whites until firm
  3. Mix chocolate melted on steam, egg whites and chopped walnuts into yolks and sugar mixture
  4. Put into a mold greased with butter
  5. Put to bake in oven preheated to 180 degrees
